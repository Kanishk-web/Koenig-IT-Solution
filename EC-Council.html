History of EC-Council

History


Founded in 2001, EC-Council (International Council of E-Commerce Consultants) has established itself as a leader in cybersecurity certification . Renowned for its innovative
programs like Certified Ethical Hacker (CEH) and Computer Hacking Forensic Investigator (CHFI) , EC-Council addresses the growing demand for skilled cybersecurity
professionals. The organization plays a pivotal role in setting industry standards and has been recognized by the United States Department of Defense among others.

EC-Council's impact on cybersecurity education is global, with its programs offered in over 145 countries. The council's commitment to counteracting cyber threats has led to the
development of cutting-edge courses that equip professionals with practical skills and real-world expertise . As cyber challenges evolve, EC-Council continues to innovate,
providing up-to-date training and certifications that are crucial for maintaining cybersecurity resilience and protecting digital infrastructure . This commitment to excellence
makes EC-Council a trusted name in IT security training .
