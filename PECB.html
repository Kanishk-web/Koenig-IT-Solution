History of PECB

History of PECB


PECB (Professional Evaluation and Certification Board) is a globally recognized certification body providing excellence and expertise in offering ISO certifications and training
across a wide range of disciplines. Since its inception, PECB has demonstrated a commitment to innovation by continually enhancing its educational programs and certification
services.

With a focus on developing standards, testing , and certification , PECB ensures that organizations and professionals are equipped with the latest knowledge and best practices
in the IT and security fields. The organization has made significant strides in shaping the landscape of IT governance and risk management through its comprehensive training
courses .

PECB's dedication to maintaining high-quality standards and fostering continuous improvement has made it a prominent name among IT professionals seeking to advance their
skills. By offering a diverse range of certifications, including ISO 27001 for information security and ISO 22301 for business continuity, PECB is at the forefront of supporting
organizations in achieving excellence and resilience.
